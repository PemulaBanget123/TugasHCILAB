Hunter

Hunter class dragon is one of the classes in Zen DragoN. Those who are included in hunter class dragons have aggresive, merciless, and non-friendly traits. Hunter is the dragon class that is known for the strength and agility it has. Hunter class dragons are also master of stealth able to move silently through the skies and stalk their prey with unparalleled precision. Their sleek bodies and shimmering scales aid in their ability to blend seamlessly into their surroundings, making them almost invisible to the unsuspecting eye. Hunter class dragons likes to do silent flight, gliding effortlessly through the skies as they survey the world below. They also relish the thrill of a challenging hunt, where their skills are put to the test against cunning prey. Also, Hunter Dragons form deep bonds with their riders, cherishing the companionship and camaraderie that comes from a shared adventure. But, Hunter clsas dragons dislikes loud noises disrupt their concentration and can startle them, making them prefer serene environments where they can focus on their surroundings. Three favorite things to do by hunter class dragons are honing their skills, exploring new territories, and basks in the sun to rejuvenate their body.

Guardian

Guardian class dragon is one of the classes in Zen DragoN. Those who are included in guardian class dragons have calm, optimist, merciful, and friendly traits. Guardian is the dragon class that is known for the defense and robust it has. Guardian class dragons are dedicated to guard due to their protective instinct. Their sense of duty and loyalty is unmatched, making them formidable defenders in times of need. With their towering stature and formidable wingspan, Guardian Dragons command respect wherever they go. Their mere presence instills a sense of awe and reassurance in those who stand beside them. Beyond their physical prowess, Guardian Dragons are known for their wisdom and insight. They possess a deep understanding of the world around them, often offering sage advice to those who seek their counsel. Guardian Dragons take great pride in protecting the weak and vulnerable, whether it be fellow dragons, other creatures, or the inhabitants of the lands they inhabit. Guardian class also cherish moments of peace and tranquility, striving to maintain harmony within their territories and beyond. Guardian Dragons value the bonds they form with their allies, cherishing the camaraderie and mutual respect that comes from fighting side by side. But then, Guardian Dragons have little tolerance for injustice and oppression, often intervening to right the wrongs they see in the world. Also, They despise betrayal and treachery, viewing it as the ultimate betrayal of their values and principles. Amazingly, Guardian Dragons are diligent in their training, constantly honing their combat skills and strategizing for the battles that lie ahead. Lastly, Guardian Dragons delight in fostering growth and development, whether it be nurturing young dragons or mentoring aspiring heroes in the ways of courage and honor.
